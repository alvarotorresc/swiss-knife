Swiss Knife — Random Tools

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Swiss Knife is a collection of random tools that works entirely offline. Your privacy is important to us, and we designed this app with a simple principle: we don't collect any data.

Data Collection

Swiss Knife does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No third-party SDKs or advertising frameworks

Internet Access

Swiss Knife does not require an internet connection. All tools work completely offline on your device.

Permissions

Swiss Knife does not request any special device permissions.

Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected, this app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
